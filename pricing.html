    Key Features

    Easy Import

    Use our Excel template or CSV import to get all your items into GearLocker in seconds.

    Add Photos

    Upload an image per item—uniform, helmet, etc.—so coaches know exactly what to issue.

    Categorize

    Organize by sport, season, size, or custom tags for quick filtering.

    History Tracking

    See at-a-glance who added or changed an item and when—team-level audit trail built in.

    Quick Assign

    Search by name, jersey number, or ID tag to issue multiple items at once.

    Team & Player Views

    Coaches can see rosters, gear status, and swap between their teams in seconds.

    Condition Tracking

    Record the state of each item at checkout to ensure accountability on return.

    Bulk Issue

    Hand out an entire set of gear to a player or batch-assign items to a whole team.

    Condition Reports

    Filter by “needs repair” or “replace” to plan upcoming orders.

    Equity & Compliance

    Compare purchase history across teams to ensure fair distribution of resources.

    Missing & Overdue Alerts

    See which players haven’t returned items and send parent notifications with one click.

    Historical Archive

    Track retired items and graduating players for complete year-over-year history.

    Purchase Requests

    Coaches submit detailed requests with item links, quantities, and costs.

    Forecasting Tools

    Use current condition and roster sizes to predict future needs.

    Live Budgets

    Push program budgets into GearLocker and watch them update automatically as purchases are approved.

    Multiple Budget Types

    Manage rollover and fixed budgets with ease.
